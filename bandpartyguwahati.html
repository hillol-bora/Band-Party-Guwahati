About Band Party Ghy

Band Party Ghy is Guwahati's premier band service, specializing in providing high-quality musical performances for all types of celebrations. With over 10 years of experience, our talented musicians bring energy, professionalism, and cultural authenticity to every event.

Based in the heart of Assam, we understand the musical traditions and preferences of the region, making us the perfect choice for your weddings, Durga Puja celebrations, Bihu festivals, and other special occasions.
